### 📡 Bachelor's Thesis – Telecommunications Engineering  
**Design and Fabrication of Very Small RFID Tag Antennas**

This project involves the **design, simulation, and fabrication of compact RFID tag antennas**.  
The goal is to achieve reliable performance while minimizing antenna size.

**Key topics:**
- RFID systems
- Antenna design and radiation
- Electromagnetic simulation
- Fabrication and testing
- Trade-offs between size and performance

This work gave me strong experience in **RF design**, **electromagnetics**, and **practical engineering constraints**.
